## Overview

This paper investigates the axial bearing capacity of bored pile foundations installed in tuff soil, using Pile Driving Analyzer (PDA) test results from the construction of ITERA's Engineering Laboratory 3 building.

## Key Findings

- Evaluated the performance of bored piles in volcanic tuff deposits characteristic of the Lampung region
- Compared PDA-based dynamic load test results with static analysis predictions
- Provided recommendations for foundation design in similar geological conditions
