## Overview

This paper compares two approaches for analyzing jetty structures: the conventional fixity point method and a more rigorous soil-structure interaction approach using spring models.

## Key Findings

- Demonstrated significant differences in predicted structural response between the two methods
- The spring model captures more realistic pile-soil interaction effects
- Provided practical guidance for marine structure design
