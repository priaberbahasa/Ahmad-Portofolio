## Overview

This thesis uses numerical simulation to study how slope geometry parameters and groundwater table position affect the magnitude and pattern of lateral displacement in liquefiable slopes during seismic loading.

## Methodology

- Parametric numerical study using finite element methods
- Systematic variation of slope geometry and groundwater table depth
- Analysis of displacement patterns under representative seismic loading
